Did You Know?

Just like a cent is the smallest unit of USD, a satoshi is the smallest unit of bitcoin, thus being the smallest denomination with which transactions can be carried out. This unit is referred to as satoshi to serve as a reference to Satoshi Nakamoto, the originator(s) of Bitcoin.
